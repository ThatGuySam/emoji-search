When iOS emoji search fails your phrase intent, do this:

1. Re-check keyboard language and input settings.
2. Test in two apps to isolate app-specific behavior.
3. Retry with shorter words, then phrase-level meaning.
4. If phrase intent is still weak, use FetchMoji and search directly by meaning.

This gives you a reliable fallback while system-level search behavior catches up.
