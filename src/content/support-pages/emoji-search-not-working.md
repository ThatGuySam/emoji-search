If emoji search stops being useful, check these in order:

1. Close and reopen your keyboard app.
2. Restart the app where you are typing.
3. Update your device OS and keyboard app.
4. Test with a simple keyword first (`heart`, `laugh`, `sad`) to verify search
   is functioning at all.

If results still feel off, the quickest workaround is semantic search by
meaning in FetchMoji.
