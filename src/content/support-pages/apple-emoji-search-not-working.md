If Apple emoji search misses what you mean:

1. Restart the app and keyboard surface.
2. Confirm your OS is current.
3. Try direct emoji names, then plain-language phrase intent.

When phrase intent still does not map, use FetchMoji as a fallback that searches
by meaning instead of strict keyword labels.
