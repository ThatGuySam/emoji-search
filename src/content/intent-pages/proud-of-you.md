When telling someone "proud of you," you usually want warmth, not hype.

Most heartfelt: `🥹`.  
Steady encouragement: `🙌`.  
Close personal support: `🫶`.

For grief or recovery contexts, pair with softer language and avoid irony-heavy
emojis.
