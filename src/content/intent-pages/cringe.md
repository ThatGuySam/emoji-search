Cringe reactions land best when the emoji matches how sharp you want to sound.

Low heat: `😬` when you want to stay playful.  
Sharper read: `😖` when you want clear discomfort.  
More exaggerated: `🫣` when you want a "can't watch" tone.

If your goal is support rather than judgment, shift to a softer emoji set.
