Use awkward emojis when you want to acknowledge tension without escalating it.

Default pick: start with `😬` for mild social friction.  
Softer pick: `😶` if you want neutral distance.  
Heavier pick: `😳` when you want to signal you felt the awkwardness directly.

If your message is about someone else doing something uncomfortable, switch to
the **secondhand embarrassment** page for a clearer tone.
