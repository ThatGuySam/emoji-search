Awkward silence usually needs an emoji that breaks tension without sounding
aggressive.

Best opener: `😶` for the literal silence vibe.  
Most common social reset: `😬`.  
If you want to soften and move on: `😅`.

If the silence is because you misspoke, pair this with **my bad** phrasing
instead of doubling down.
