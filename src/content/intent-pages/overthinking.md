Overthinking emojis work best when they acknowledge mental load without making
the message unreadable.

Balanced pick: `🤔`.  
Spiral feeling: `😵‍💫`.  
Light self-aware tone: `🫠`.

If the tone is intentionally playful or delusional-on-purpose, check **delulu**
for better stylistic fit.
