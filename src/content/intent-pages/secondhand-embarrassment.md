Secondhand embarrassment reactions often need a mix of discomfort and humor.

Best direct fit: `🫣`.  
Classic awkward overlap: `😬`.  
Stronger discomfort: `😖`.

If your audience might take it as criticism, lean toward `😬` and softer
wording.
